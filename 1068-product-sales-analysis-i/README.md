<pre style="position: relative; user-select: auto;">+--------------+---------+
| Column Name  | Type    |
+--------------+---------+

<p style="user-select: auto;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">Write an SQL query that reports the <code style="user-select: auto;">product_name</code>, <code style="user-select: auto;">year</code>, and <code style="user-select: auto;">price</code> for each <code style="user-select: auto;">sale_id</code> in the <code style="user-select: auto;">Sales</code> table.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">Return the resulting table in <strong style="user-select: auto;">any order</strong>.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">The query result format is in the following example.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="user-select: auto;"><strong class="example" style="user-select: auto;">Example 1:</strong></p>

<pre style="position: relative; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input:</strong> 
Sales table:
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+
| sale_id | product_id | year | quantity | price |
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+ 
| 1       | 100        | 2008 | 10       | 5000  |
| 2       | 100        | 2009 | 12       | 5000  |
| 7       | 200        | 2011 | 15       | 9000  |
+---------+------------+------+----------+-------+
Product table:
+------------+--------------+
| product_id | product_name |
+------------+--------------+
| 100        | Nokia        |
| 200        | Apple        |
| 300        | Samsung      |
+------------+--------------+
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Output:</strong> 
+--------------+-------+-------+
| product_name | year  | price |
+--------------+-------+-------+
| Nokia        | 2008  | 5000  |
| Nokia        | 2009  | 5000  |
| Apple        | 2011  | 9000  |
+--------------+-------+-------+
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation:</strong> 
From sale_id = 1, we can conclude that Nokia was sold for 5000 in the year 2008.
From sale_id = 2, we can conclude that Nokia was sold for 5000 in the year 2009.
From sale_id = 7, we can conclude that Apple was sold for 9000 in the year 2011.
